## Sales Tools

Your manager will provision the following tools during your first week:
- 

**HubSpot**: CRM and system of record. All pipeline, accounts, and deal tracking happens in HubSpot.
- 

**Gong**: Call recording. All external calls should be recorded.
- 

**SalesLoft**: Sales engagement and outbound sequencing. All prospecting runs through SalesLoft.
- 

**LinkedIn Sales Navigator**: Prospecting and research.
- 

**Seismic**: Sales content and enablement. Decks, collateral, and competitive intel live here.

## Client Entertainment

- 

Prospect/customer meals: Up to $100/person.
- 

No executive dinner budget. All client meals follow the same $100/person cap.
- 

Gifts to prospects: Up to $50/person. Log with your manager via email.

## Expense Reporting

Submit all client entertainment receipts via Concur within 10 business days. Include the attendee list and business purpose.
## CRM Expectations

- 

Update HubSpot after every customer touchpoint.
- 

All deals need a close date and a next step.

## First Two Weeks

- 

Week 1: Tool setup, shadow calls, review the pitch deck in Seismic.
- 

Week 2: Practice pitch, begin outbound in SalesLoft, attend pipeline review.
