## Core Systems

All employees are provisioned with the following accounts on their first day.
### Communication

- 

**Google Workspace** (Gmail, Calendar, Drive): Primary email and document platform.
- 

**Slack**: Real-time messaging. Join #general and your team channel.
- 

**Zoom**: Video conferencing for all meetings.

### Project Management

- 

**Asana**: Company-wide task management. All teams use Asana for project tracking and cross-functional work.
- 

**Confluence**: Internal wiki and knowledge base.

### Security

- 

**Okta**: Single sign-on portal. All tools are accessible from your Okta dashboard.
- 

**LastPass**: Shared password manager. Your manager will invite you to the appropriate shared folders.
- 

**Symantec Endpoint Protection**: Antivirus. Pre-installed on your laptop. Do not uninstall.

### HR

- 

**Gusto**: Payroll, benefits, and PTO. Complete your tax forms and benefits enrollment within your first 3 business days.

## Laptop Specs

- 

Engineering: MacBook Pro 14" M2, 32GB RAM, 512GB storage.
- 

All other teams: MacBook Air 13" M2, 8GB RAM, 256GB storage.

## Password Policy

- 

Minimum 12 characters.
- 

MFA required on Okta. Use Google Authenticator or SMS verification.
