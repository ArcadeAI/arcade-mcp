## Travel Booking

- 

**Flights**: Economy for all domestic. Business class permitted for international flights over 6 hours with manager approval.
- 

**Hotels**: Up to $200/night domestic. No high-cost city exceptions.
- 

**Booking**: Use Concur for all flights and hotels. Access via Okta.

## Meals

- 

Travel meals: Up to $60/day domestic, $80/day international. Alcohol is not reimbursable.
- 

Team dinners: Up to $30/person. Manager approval for groups over 4.

## Expense Reporting

- 

All receipts must be submitted via Concur within 10 business days.
- 

Expenses over $250 require manager approval.
- 

Missing receipts: file a missing receipt form in Concur for expenses over $50.

## Corporate Card

All employees will receive an Amex corporate card. Use it for all business travel. Personal card usage will not be reimbursed except in emergencies.
