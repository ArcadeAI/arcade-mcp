# Sales Expense & Client Entertainment Policy
**Last updated: January 2026** **Owner: Sales Operations / Finance** **Public URL:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jDOjxYF8naUUelpkFo4JUaAP9qc6RasnEBnpiZrUrMg/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.x3ivp9hfpacc  **Status: CURRENT**
## Overview
Sales team members have expanded expense authority to support pipeline development and customer relationships. This policy supplements the company-wide Travel & Expense Policy with sales-specific guidelines.
## Client Meals & Entertainment
- **Prospect/customer meals**: Up to $150/person. No pre-approval required for groups of 4 or fewer. Groups of 5+ require manager approval.
- **Executive dinners**: Up to $250/person. VP Sales approval required. Must include at least one VP or C-level prospect attendee.
- **Event hosting** (happy hours, dinners tied to conferences): Up to $2,000 total per event. VP Sales and Finance approval required. Submit a brief plan in advance via #ask-sales-ops.
- **Gifts to prospects/customers**: Up to $100/person, max 2 per account per quarter. No cash or cash equivalents. Log in Salesforce under the account record.
## Expense Documentation
All client-facing expenses require the following in Ramp:

- Receipt
- Attendee names and titles
- Company names
- Business purpose (1 sentence: e.g., "Pipeline development dinner with [Account] -- discussing Q2 expansion")

Expenses missing attendee details will be rejected by Finance.
## Travel for Customer Meetings
Sales travel follows the company-wide Travel & Expense Policy (book via Navan, use Ramp). Additional sales-specific guidance:

- Same-day customer visits within your region do not require pre-approval.
- Overnight travel for customer meetings requires manager approval.
- Travel to close a deal at a prospect's office is encouraged. Use good judgment on frequency.
## What Is Not Covered
- Personal meals while traveling (use the standard $75/day per diem from the Travel & Expense Policy).
- Entertainment without a clear business purpose.
- Alcohol-only tabs. Alcohol must accompany a meal.
- Gifts exceeding $100 or gifts of cash/gift cards.
