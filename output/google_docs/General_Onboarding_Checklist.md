# General Onboarding Checklist
**Last updated: February 2026** **Owner: People Operations** **Public URL:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1fP6v0D_vm81sdLdEJ-R6x4zcDSJ72Mga1safNQ2piDE/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.mhaig1vrtdn  **Status: CURRENT**
## Welcome to Acme Corp!
This checklist covers everything you need to complete during your first week. Your manager and onboarding buddy will guide you through each step, but you are responsible for completing all items by end of Day 5.
## Day 1: Accounts & Access
1. Pick up your laptop and peripherals from the IT window on the 2nd floor (Suite 210). Bring your government-issued photo ID.
1. Log into Okta (our SSO provider) using the credentials emailed to your personal address. Okta is the gateway to all other systems, so start here.
1. From Okta, access the following and confirm you can log in:
  - Gmail / Google Workspace
  - Slack (join #general, #announcements, and your team channel)
  - Jira
  - Confluence
  - 1Password (accept vault invitations from your manager)
  - BambooHR (complete your personal profile and tax documents)
1. Set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) on Okta using the Okta Verify app on your personal phone. MFA is mandatory and must be configured before end of Day 1.
## Day 1: HR & Compliance
1. Complete your I-9 verification in BambooHR. You must bring acceptable identity documents on your first day.
1. Review and sign the Employee Handbook acknowledgment in BambooHR.
1. Complete the following compliance training modules in BambooHR Learning:
  - Information Security Awareness (30 min)
  - Anti-Harassment & Workplace Conduct (45 min)
  - Data Privacy & GDPR Basics (20 min)
1. Enroll in benefits within your first 5 business days via BambooHR. The benefits guide is pinned in #people-ops on Slack.
## Day 2-3: Orientation Sessions
Your calendar will be pre-loaded with the following:

- Company All-Hands (if your start date falls on a Monday)
- Product Overview with the PM team
- Security & Acceptable Use Policy walkthrough with IT
- 1:1 with your manager to review your 30/60/90 day plan
## Day 4-5: Team-Specific Onboarding
Your manager will provide a team-specific onboarding doc with tools, repos, access, and workflows relevant to your role. Complete all items by end of your first week.
## Onboarding Buddy
You will be assigned an onboarding buddy from outside your immediate team. They will reach out on Slack within your first day. Buy them a coffee -- expense it on Ramp.
## Welcome Email
HR sends a welcome email to all new hires at their personal email address at least 2 business days before their start date.

The email should be concise and scannable. Use bold all-caps section headers and bullet points. Do not use prose paragraphs. Sign off as the hiring manager, not People Ops.
### Welcome Email Assembly
When drafting a welcome email for a new hire, include the following sections in order. Use the new hire's details to fill in the blanks.

**All hires — always include these sections in this order:**

1. LAPTOP & BUILDING
1. ACCOUNTS
1. HR
1. KEY DOCS

**Engineering hires — insert between HR and KEY DOCS:**

- ENGINEERING SETUP (see Engineering Onboarding Guide for exact content)

**Sales hires — insert between HR and KEY DOCS:**

- SALES SETUP (see Sales Onboarding Guide for exact content)
### KEY DOCS by role
Every welcome email must include links to the following docs. URLs are in each doc's header.

**All hires:**

- General Onboarding Checklist
- IT Systems & Access Guide

**Engineering hires (append to above):**

- Engineering Onboarding Guide
- Engineering Security & Compliance

**Sales hires (append to above):**

- Sales Onboarding Guide

## Slack Introduction
On your first day, HR will post a welcome message in #general to introduce you to the company, including a callout to your manager. Once you're in Slack, post your own introduction in #new-hires using the template below:

Hi everyone! I'm [Name], joining as [Role] on the [Team] team, based in [Location]. Before Acme, I was at [Previous Company/School]. Fun fact: [Something interesting about you]. Excited to be here!
