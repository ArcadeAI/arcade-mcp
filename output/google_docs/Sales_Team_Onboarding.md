# Sales Team Onboarding
**Last updated: February 2026** **Owner: VP Sales** **Public URL:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/141lz59RWPiPm-_EOzIogDqlCpJjscvJ7QYyD5QfWHsg/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.ctz2it8emav3  **Status: CURRENT**
## Welcome to Acme Sales
This document supplements the General Onboarding Checklist with sales-specific tools, access, and norms. Complete the general checklist first, then work through this doc.
## Welcome Email Additions
When drafting the welcome email for a sales hire, append the following section after HR and before KEY DOCS:

**SALES SETUP**

- Okta will provision your core sales tools: Salesforce, Gong, Outreach, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Clari, and Highspot.
- Install the Gong browser extension and Zoom integration on Day 1.
- Your manager will assign your territory and account list within your first 3 days.

Also append to KEY DOCS:

- Sales Onboarding Guide
## Sales Tools (provisioned via Okta)
- **Salesforce**: CRM and system of record for all pipeline, accounts, and opportunities. Your manager will assign your territory and account list within your first 3 days.
- **Gong**: Call recording and conversation intelligence. All external calls must be recorded via Gong. Install the Gong browser extension and Zoom integration on Day 1.
- **Outreach**: Sales engagement and sequencing. All outbound prospecting runs through Outreach. Do not use personal email or standalone tools for outbound.
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator**: Prospecting and account research. Licensed seats are provisioned by your manager.
- **Clari**: Forecasting and pipeline inspection. Updated weekly by each AE. Your manager will walk you through forecast methodology in Week 1.
- **Highspot**: Sales content and enablement. Pitch decks, one-pagers, case studies, and competitive battle cards live here. Do not create or share sales materials outside Highspot.
## Slack Channels
- #team-sales: General sales discussion.
- #sales-wins: Celebrate closed deals here.
- #sales-enablement: New content, training, and competitive updates.
- #ask-sales-ops: Questions on Salesforce, territories, commission, quoting.
## CRM Hygiene
- Update Salesforce within 24 hours of any customer interaction (calls, emails, meetings).
- All deals must have: a close date, a next step, and an identified champion. Deals without a next step for 14+ days will be flagged by Sales Ops.
- Stage progression must follow the defined sales process. Do not skip stages.
## First Two Weeks
- Week 1: Complete tool setup, shadow 3 discovery calls and 2 demo calls, review the sales playbook in Highspot, 1:1 with your manager to review territory and quota.
- Week 2: Deliver your first practice pitch to your manager, begin outbound prospecting via Outreach, attend your first pipeline review.
- End of Week 2: Certification call with Sales Enablement (you must pass before running live calls solo).
## Commission & Quota
Your commission plan and quota details will be shared by Sales Ops during Week 1. Direct questions to #ask-sales-ops or your manager.
