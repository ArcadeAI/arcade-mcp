# Travel & Expense Policy
**Last updated: March 2026** **Owner: Finance** **Public URL:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/14GUEaRC48vki9wIxzTZJB1vgOFAvcnohlZZmW4IXrVA/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.dmz1l4zg5721  **Status: CURRENT**
## General Principles
Acme Corp will reimburse reasonable business expenses. Use good judgment. Spend company money as if it were your own. When in doubt, ask your manager before spending.
## Ramp Corporate Card
All full-time employees will receive a virtual Ramp card. Use it for all business expenses. Personal credit cards should only be used if Ramp is temporarily unavailable, and must be reconciled within 10 business days.
## Travel Booking
- **Flights**: Book economy class for domestic flights under 5 hours. Premium economy is permitted for flights over 5 hours. Business class requires VP-level approval for domestic and is permitted for international flights over 8 hours.
- **Hotels**: Up to $250/night for domestic travel. Up to $350/night for SF, NYC, Seattle, and other high-cost cities. International per diems vary by country -- check the rate table in Confluence.
- **Ground Transportation**: Use rideshare (Uber/Lyft) for airport transfers and local travel. Rental cars require manager approval and are generally limited to situations where rideshare is impractical.
- **Booking Platform**: Use Navan (accessible via Okta) for all flights and hotels. Bookings outside Navan require a written exception from Finance.
## Meals & Incidentals
- **Travel meals**: Up to $75/day domestic, $100/day international. Alcohol is capped at $25/person/day.
- **Team meals (local)**: Up to $40/person for team dinners. Manager approval required for groups over 6. VP approval required for groups over 15.
- **Client entertainment**: Up to $150/person. Director-level approval required. Log the attendees, company names, and business purpose in Ramp.
## Expense Reporting
- All receipts must be uploaded to Ramp within 5 business days of the transaction.
- Expenses over $500 require manager approval in Ramp.
- Expenses over $5,000 require VP approval.
- Missing receipts: If you lose a receipt, submit a Missing Receipt Affidavit (template in Ramp) for any expense over $25. Two or more missing receipts in a quarter will trigger a review.
## Non-Reimbursable Expenses
The following are not reimbursable: personal travel extensions, in-flight Wi-Fi for personal use, hotel minibars, gym or spa charges, first-class or business-class upgrades not pre-approved, traffic or parking violations, and pet care.
## Conference & Event Attendance
Conferences require manager approval. If the ticket exceeds $1,000, VP approval is also needed. Acme Corp will cover the ticket, travel, and lodging per the rates above. Submit a brief post-event summary to your manager within 5 business days.
