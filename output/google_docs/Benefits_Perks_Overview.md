# Benefits & Perks Overview
**Last updated: February 2026** **Owner: People Operations** **Public URL:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/19kZ3g7SqhYOsLZTyjUC-tHNLBIf-UtQohsvNSatmjoI/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.x2ywq35775v9  **Status: CURRENT**
## Benefits Enrollment
You must enroll in benefits within your first 5 business days via BambooHR. If you miss the enrollment window, you will need to wait until the next open enrollment period (November) unless you experience a qualifying life event.
## Health Insurance
- **Medical**: Acme offers three plans via Aetna: a PPO, an HDHP with HSA, and an HMO (CA employees only). Acme covers 90% of the employee premium and 70% of dependent premiums.
- **Dental**: Delta Dental PPO. Acme covers 100% of the employee premium.
- **Vision**: VSP. Acme covers 100% of the employee premium.

Plan details, formularies, and provider networks are in the Benefits Guide pinned in #people-ops on Slack.
## 401(k)
- Acme offers a 401(k) through Guideline. Acme matches 100% of contributions up to 4% of your salary.
- You are automatically enrolled at a 6% contribution rate. Adjust via Guideline (accessible through BambooHR).
- Vesting: immediate for your contributions, 1-year cliff for employer match.
## Paid Time Off
- **PTO**: Acme has a flexible (unlimited) PTO policy. We ask that you take at least 15 days per year. Notify your manager at least 2 weeks in advance for absences of 3+ days.
- **Sick leave**: Separate from PTO. Use BambooHR to log sick days. No prior approval needed.
- **Holidays**: Acme observes 11 company holidays per year. The holiday calendar is in Google Calendar (subscribed automatically).
- **Parental leave**: 16 weeks fully paid for all new parents, regardless of gender. Notify People Ops at least 30 days in advance.
## Additional Perks
- **Learning & Development**: $1,500/year per employee for courses, books, and conferences (separate from conference travel, which is covered under the Travel & Expense Policy).
- **Home Office Stipend**: $500 one-time for remote employees to set up their home workspace.
- **Commuter Benefits**: Pre-tax commuter benefits via BambooHR for transit and parking.
- **Gym Membership**: Acme reimburses up to $75/month for gym or fitness memberships. Submit receipts in Ramp.
- **Mental Health**: Free access to Headspace and 6 free therapy sessions per year via Lyra Health.
- **Cell Phone**: $50/month stipend for all employees, added to payroll.
