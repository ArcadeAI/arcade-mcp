# Office & Facilities Guide
**Last updated: March 2026** **Owner: Office Operations** **Public URL:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1VDdLu0co-kw6wnrRNlXc_sZlJTl-6CkEMIBBrVn1ksQ/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.6xp9j5iqhbwx  **Status: CURRENT**
## Office Location
Acme Corp HQ is at 100 Innovation Drive, Suite 400, San Francisco, CA 94105. The office entrance is on the 4th floor -- take the elevators from the main lobby.
## Building Access
- You will receive a Verkada keycard on your first day. Pick it up from the front desk.
- The office is accessible 7am to 9pm Monday through Friday. After-hours access requires manager approval submitted via #office-ops on Slack.
- Lost keycards: report immediately to #office-ops. A replacement will be issued within 1 business day. Your old card will be deactivated instantly.
## Office Layout
- **4th Floor**: Engineering, Product, Design. Includes 4 phone booths, 2 large conference rooms (Tesla, Turing), and the main kitchen.
- **5th Floor**: Marketing, Sales, GTM, People Ops, Finance. Includes 3 phone booths, 2 conference rooms (Lovelace, Hopper), and a secondary kitchen.
- **Quiet Zone**: The east wing on both floors is designated as a quiet zone. No calls, no speakerphone, no group conversations.
## Conference Rooms
Book via Google Calendar. Rooms are equipped with Zoom Rooms hardware (Logitech Rally cameras, Jabra speakers). If something is not working, post in #office-ops. Do not attempt to rewire AV equipment yourself.

Maximum booking: 1 hour. Back-to-back bookings by the same person are discouraged. Release your room if your meeting ends early.
## Kitchen & Meals
- **Lunch**: Provided daily via Forkable. Place your order by 10am. Orders not placed by the deadline will not be fulfilled.
- **Snacks & Beverages**: Stocked weekly. Requests can be posted in #office-snack-requests.
- **Coffee**: La Marzocca espresso machines on both floors. Clean up after yourself.
- **Dishes**: Load the dishwasher. Do not leave dirty dishes in the sink. The dish fairy does not work here.
## Parking
Garage parking is available at the building. Acme Corp subsidizes monthly parking passes at $150/month (the building charges $300). Contact #office-ops to set up payroll deduction.
## Mail & Shipping
Personal packages may be sent to the office. Outgoing FedEx/UPS: supplies and pre-paid labels are at the front desk. For business shipments, coordinate with #office-ops.
